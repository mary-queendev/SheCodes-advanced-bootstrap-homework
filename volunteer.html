I am a Serial Volunteer and Advocate for the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

I volunteer in service to humanity to support the attainment of the UN SDGs.
I volunteered as the Programs Lead on Climate Action at Emandes Social Development Network Abuja and built my skills in designing and managing programs on climate action and environmental sustainability. I also implemented and managed projects on climate action including online advocacy and grassroot community sensitization projects; and Coordinated partnerships with external partners/ stakeholders on Climate action projects. I also represented the organisation as a Delegate/ speaker for Climate action (SDG 13) at the SDGs Connect summit, Nigeria 2023. Find snippet of my speech here; Delegate/ Speaker for SDG 13 (Climate action)

One of my notable initiatives was the sustainable alternatives to plastic bags "SEAP Bags"project- an online advocacy and grassroot community engagement campaign which involved climate and environmental experts impacting over 2000 people and received wide acceptance as 65kg of plastic wastes were collected for recycling. I dedicated my National Youth service (NYSC) to volunteering for the SDGs and served as the President of the NYSC SDGs Community Development Service group in Kano state, Nigeria.
This gave me a platform to build my leadership skills as I led a team of over 100 youths across 15 local government areas in Kano state to sucessfully executing over seventeen (17) SDG projects in the state.

    Some of the projects include;

  • Online SDG advocacy
  • Community sensitization on the SDGs
  • Tree planting for climate action
  • Environmental Sanitation
  • Skill acquisition for students, women and girls
  • Secondary and Primary school quiz competitions
  • Donations to orphanages, home for disabled, amongst others.

    These volunteering experiences further built my competencies in

  • Leadership
  • Projects/ programs management
  • Excellent Team work and collaboration
  • People management
  • Public speaking
  • Problem solving
  • Critical thinking
  • Excellent Time management and work delivery
  • Event management and compereing
